## 1. IndiaAI Innovation Challenge 2026 (AP Governance Track) — TOP PRIORITY

**What:** Government of India challenge in partnership with AP's RTGS. Specifically seeks AI solutions for AP governance.

**Why this is #1:** IndiaAI + RTGS (who runs all 11,162 secretariats) are the co-hosts. Your product directly solves the governance problem they listed. No other applicant targets secretariat employees.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Portal** | https://aikosh.indiaai.gov.in/home/competitions/all |
| **Challenge Page** | https://indiaai.gov.in/article/indiaai-innovation-challenge-for-transforming-governance-with-government-of-andhra-pradesh |
| **Stage 2 Funding** | **INR 25 Lakh** per selected team (up to 3 teams per problem statement) |
| **Stage 3 Contract** | **Up to INR 1 Crore** (2-year deployment contract with government) |
| **Query Email** | fellow3.gpai-india@meity.gov.in (Subject: "Query- IndiaAI Transforming Governance Challenge 2026 – [Your Name]") |
| **What to Submit** | Team Info, Project Proposal, Business Plan, Pitch Deck, Certificate of Incorporation (or intent to register), Demo Video |

### How to Position Your Application

The challenge has 6 problem statements (supply chain, solar land, urban planning, school infrastructure, etc.). Your product doesn't fit these exactly — but the overarching theme is **"AI for Transforming Governance in AP"**, and the challenge description says: *"The Challenge aims to identify and deploy high-potential AI solutions that enhance transparency, operational efficiency and inclusive growth."*

**Your pitch angle:**
> "While the listed problem statements address specific department verticals, the 1.3 lakh village secretariat employees who execute ALL these programs are themselves unsupported by AI. Our copilot is horizontal infrastructure — it makes every vertical program more efficient by giving the frontline employee instant scheme knowledge, automated paperwork, grievance tracking with SLA enforcement, and AI-powered daily task management. The Jan 2026 Yogandhra survey pressure on sachivalayam staff validates this urgency."

## What to Prepare (Common Across All Submissions)

### 1. Pitch Deck (10-12 slides)
```
Slide 1:  Title — AP Sachivalayam AI Copilot
Slide 2:  Problem — 1.3L employees, 34 depts, 100+ schemes, burnout crisis
Slide 3:  Yogandhra survey evidence — staff under pressure (cite Deccan Chronicle)
Slide 4:  Solution — 4 core features (scheme checker, form drafter, grievance, task)
Slide 5:  Demo screenshots — WhatsApp conversation flow (Telugu)
Slide 6:  Tech stack — Claude + Whisper + pgvector + GSWS API
Slide 7:  Advanced features — Rate limiting, audit trail, duplicate detection
Slide 8:  Zero competition analysis — Why nobody else is building this
Slide 9:  Pilot plan — 5 secretariats, 55 employees, 6 weeks
Slide 10: Success metrics — Time saved, burnout reduction, citizen wait time
Slide 11: Scaling plan — District → State → 1.3L employees in 12 months
Slide 12: Team + Ask
```

- Employee sends WhatsApp message in Telugu

- Voice note → transcription → form auto-fill → PDF generation
- Grievance filing → SLA tracking → escalation notification
- Task plan → daily prioritization → "task done" completion
- Show the analytics dashboard API responses
